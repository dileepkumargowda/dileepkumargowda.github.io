Fixed wakeup time

Fixing a time for a sleeping will be always better than sleeping at the time when body calls for. if you are not able to go to bed at same time everyday, atlest fix the time you wake up everyday no matter how much less sleep you get that day, and you can always catch up your sleep later in the day

if you are not sleep deprived for continueous two to four days you can still manage less of sleep in one night and still do your worldly affairs quite well as usual.

However if you distract your self everyday your fixed time of waking up it distrubs your way of starting a day it intern impact your day productivity towords your fixed current goal in sight.

Follow the same timing no matter what and where you are. if you are in the place where you have nothing to do waking up early then just go for a walk, or sit on your bed start revisiting your last day activities mentally or practice gratittude listings whatever. Idea here is you wake up everyday fixed time no matter what, even you got just two hours sleep in that perticular day.
